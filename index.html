About Airworthy

Growing up in Utah as an underground hip-hop head, Airworthy found inspiration from groups like Atmosphere, Cunninlynguists and Hieroglyphics. His music career officially launched in 2015 with ProVeracity, a group of talented childhood friends consisting of Airworthy, IMPAKT, and Acrophile. With over 2 years of experience living in Japan and fluency in the language, many Japanese cultural influences can be found in his and ProVeracity's tracks, including multilingual lyricism. Airworthy is also known for his music videos with collab partner and YouTube influencer, Chase Dahl with over 30k subscribers.
