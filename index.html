About Airworthy

Growing up in Utah as an underground hiphop head, Airworthy found inspiration from groups like Atmosphere, Binary Star and Hieroglyphics. His music career was launched in 2015 with ProVeracity, a group formed by him and childhood friends, IMPAKT and Acrophile. Airworthy is fluent in Japanese and has lived in Japan for over 2 years. His connection to Japan, including Japanese lyrics are found in several of his and ProVeracity's tracks.
